Our Executive Team

At 3DTrance, we have experienced, diligent, passionate and dedicated outstanding professionals from various fields who love what they do and live it. The 3DTrance team is an oiled, smooth machine, where each member complements each others skills and expertise.
We are here to use our skills and expertise to bring all your ideas, sketches and imagination to life through visually stimulating images, videos, 360o panoramas, Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), Holographic Reality (HR) and Interactive display.

  • Afolabi, Olalekan Managing Director
    Architect, Project Manager & Visualization expert...
    Olalekan Afolabi is the Managing Partner at 3DTrance. He has a keen eye for details, and is a pro at graphic design, storyboarding critics. He is also an animation lover (cartoons!)
    Afolabi Olalekan gained his 1st degree from University of Lagos, Akoka. He also finished a master mind series from NIIT, Victoria Island, Lagos and a course on Innovation Management from Akure Business School, Akure, Ondo.
    He loves visualization a great deal, so he spends his spare time traveling to various famous architectural landmarks world over.
  • Ajadi, Stephen Creative Director
    Artist, Poet, Architect...
    Stephen Ajadi is a Partner in 3DTrance. He is a renowned artist, writer, researcher, a poet and art lover.
    Stephen Ajadi gained his 1st degree from Ladoke Akintola University of Technology, Ogbomosho (LAUTECH) and his 2nd degree from University of Lagos, Akoka (UNILAG).
    He has a master’s degree from Architects Association School, London (A.A School), a Master’s degree in design and business from the Cranfield University, London and ESTM Master’s Degree from Southern California Institute of Architecture, Los Angeles, USA. He holds various workshops called WORKSHOP ZERO all over the world. He is the founder of African Contemporary Institute of Design (A.C.I.D) an institute that teaches world class in-depth knowledge of what Architecture is.
  • Okunola, Segun Administrative Director
    Developer, Lecturer, Contractor...
    Segun Okunola is a Partner in 3DTrance, working in collaboration with his firm, New Deal.
    Segun Okunola gained his 1st degree from the Obafemi Awolowo University (O.A.U), Ile Ife. His master’s degree was also gotten from O.A.U.
    Construction expert as well as senior lecturer in the University of Lagos, He is a research expert in the area of how built environments affect the psychology of occupants. He is a Member of Nigerian Institute of Architects(N.I.A).
  • Afolabi, Funmilola Talent Manager
    Linguistics, Programming, Fashion...
    Funmilola Afolabi is our Talent Manger at 3DTrance Limited. She finished her 1st degree from the University of Lagos, Akoka and is a member of the Chartered Institute of Personnel Management, Nigeria.
    Funmilola Afolabi is a business development expert in product development and marketing, an android developer, a jewelry designer and a linguistic enthusiast.
    Afolabi Funmilola is passionate about solving problems facing the average Nigerian. She believes the nigerian youth are capable of providing innovative solutions to matters that affect the society. She pours herself into these projects, by collaborating with organizations that have these as their core objectives.
