THE ITAN

Won the 2nd Place at the CPDI Africa Design Competition 2017

Team : Olalekan Afolabi (3DTrance) & Olalekan Ileola-Gold (Soft)

An Icon African design of a mixed used development that narrate a story of unity, diverse culture, languages being brought together to bring about peace and stop xenophobia among Africans. Also celebrating the unique characteristics of Africa, a fertile woman, an endowed mother and a beauty to behold.
