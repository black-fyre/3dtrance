What we do

At 3DTrance, we believe in the power of the extra mile. As such, We go above and beyond the call of duty to make sure our clients expectations are always exceeded. Bringing to life a design from sketch, into a photorealistic medium, so it can be a guide, from which dreams can be brought into reality during implementation.

  • Design
    Design of any building typology/ elements, based on Client's Brief or Ideas, put together into a comprehensive package, in form of photorealistic images/Video with the necessary drawings
  • Facade Design
    In architecture, the fa√ßade of a building is often the most important aspect from a design standpoint, as it sets the tone for the rest of the building. We do facade design for any building Typology.
  • Axonometric Plans
    Axonometric Plans is the visualization of your plans based on your sketch, design, ideas for your spaces put together into a photorealistic images showing proposed finishes, mood & ambience.
  • Elevation Visuals
    Elevation Visuals is the schematic view of internal/external faces of any building, based on your sketch and design, into photorealistic images.
  • Exterior Visuals
    Exterior Visuals is the external 3D views of any building typology based on your sketch, design, facade, landscape put together into a photo-realistic images showing proposed finishes, mood & ambience.
  • Interior Visuals
    Interior Visuals is the visualization of any internal space based on your sketch, design, ideas for your spaces put together into a photo-realistic images showing proposed finishes, mood & ambience.
  • Animation/Walkthrough
    Animation/Walk-through of any building typology based on your sketch, design, facade, landscape put together into a photo-realistic video showing proposed finishes, mood & ambience.
  • Panorama/AR/VR/HR
    Panorama/AR/VR/HR of either an interior space or exterior scene. View from a point that shows the entire surrounding, put together into a photo-realistic images showing proposed finishes, mood & ambience.
