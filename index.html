THE ITAN

2nd place at the Design Competition by CPDI AFRICA 2017

Design by Olalekan Afolabi & Olalekan Ileola-Gold

SHORT NARRATIVE
An Icon African design of a mixed used development that narrates a story of unity, diverse culture, languages being brought together to bring about peace and stop xenophobia among Africans. Also celebrating the unique characteristics of Africa, a fertile woman, an endowed mother and a beauty to behold.

THE HALVES

... the halves of reality, only seeing one's perception

Design by Olalekan Afolabi & Visual by 3DTrance Team

SHORT NARRATIVE
A museum that tries to show the halves of realities, the halves of things seen and unseen, the halves of perception, halves of culture interpretation, halves of differences that leads to conflict and tries to bring them together, peacefully reconciling their differences based on the halves of realities they are from.

THE WAVE

... the wave of uncertainty, change being a constant

Design by Olalekan Afolabi & Visual by 3DTrance

SHORT NARRATIVE
A Multi Purpose design for event and a range of activities which was inspired by the wave of uncertainty, change being a constant in our society of today. We need spaces that are adaptable to different uses yet very functional in whatever the program of uses is. Also depicting an historical relevant of changes in time either influence by politics, culture, social interaction or technology

PLANE

... plane being an absence of depth, light is all that is needed

Design by Olalekan Afolabi & Visual by 3DTrance Team

SHORT NARRATIVE
An auditorium which can be used for various purpose inspired by the illumination of knowledge, which transform ones perception, before encounter of the light of knowledge and understanding. a plane being an absent of depth, light is all that is needed to transform a plane into a box. Also depicting the powerful effect and transformation that occurs when one is informed.
